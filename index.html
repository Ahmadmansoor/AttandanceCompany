Login/Logout Form Issues

If you're experiencing issues with the login or logout form, please try the following:

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
  • Verify that you are entering the correct username and password.
  • If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" option (if available).
  • If you are still having problems, close the app completely and reopen it.
  • Confirm that the application is updated to the latest version.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: I cannot log in. What should I do?
    A: Double-check your login credentials and internet connection. If the problem persists, contact support.
  • Q: How do I log out of the app?
    A: Simply press the "Logout" button on the main screen.
  • Q: I am having issues with the app crashing.
    A: Please make sure that your application is updated to the most recent version, and if the issue continues, please contact support.
