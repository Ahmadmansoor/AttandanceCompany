Universal Attendance Company - App Support

The Universal Attendance App is a comprehensive solution designed to enhance workforce management across organizations. It features a user-friendly mobile interface, enabling employees to record their attendance effortlessly, and a robust Windows interface for administrative tasks. Through the Windows interface, companies can define their organizational profiles, register branch locations, and manage user roles effectively. This app empowers HR departments by providing real-time data on employee check-ins and check-outs, tracking attendance across branches, and generating detailed reports on tardiness and absenteeism. By integrating mobility and centralized management, the Universal Attendance App simplifies attendance tracking and promotes organizational efficiency.

Login/Logout Form Issues

If you're experiencing issues with the login or logout form, please try the following:

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
  • Verify that you are entering the correct username and password.
  • If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" option (if available).
  • If you are still having problems, close the app completely and reopen it.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: I cannot log in. What should I do?
    A: Double-check your login credentials and internet connection. If the problem persists, contact support.
  • Q: How do I log out of the app?
    A: Simply press the "Logout" button on the main screen.
  • Q: I am having issues with the app crashing.
    A: Please make sure that your application is updated to the most recent version, and if the issue continues, please contact support.
